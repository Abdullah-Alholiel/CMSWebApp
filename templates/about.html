About OpenAI University

OpenAI University is aOpenAI is a prestigious university based in the UK, renowned for its size and diversity. Our vibrant community consists of approximately 35,000 students, 4,500 staff members, and an extensive network of 295,000 alumni worldwide. At OpenAI, our mission is straightforward yet powerful: we are dedicated to transforming lives. We believe in providing individuals from all walks of life with the opportunity to acquire the skills, knowledge, and hands-on experience they need to thrive in their chosen endeavors. As one of the UK's largest and most forward-thinking universities, we prioritize teaching, research, and partnerships that make a tangible impact on the real world. Our focus lies in addressing the pressing health, economic, and social challenges that our society faces today. We approach our work with ambition and a sense of purpose, not only for our university and its community but also for our students, colleagues, partners, city, and region. Our vision is to become the world's foremost applied university, setting an example of what can be achieved when a university is genuinely committed to transforming lives.
